BAMbs Consult Service Agreement — Terms & Conditions

This agreement is entered into between BAMbs Consult (BAc), a globally operating fund recovery consultancy, and the client named above.

1. Scope of Services. BAMbs Consult agrees to conduct a thorough investigation including fund tracing, regulatory liaison, legal coordination, and negotiation with financial institutions on behalf of the Client.

2. No Upfront Fees. BAMbs Consult does not charge any upfront, advance, or administrative fees. All fees are strictly contingency-based.

3. Success Fee. Upon successful recovery, a fee of 15% of the total recovered amount is payable, covering all investigative, legal, and administrative costs.

4. Client Obligations. The Client agrees to provide accurate and complete information, cooperate fully with the investigation, and promptly supply additional documentation when requested.

5. Confidentiality. All information shared shall remain strictly confidential and shall not be disclosed except where required by law or necessary for executing the recovery process.

6. No Guarantee. While BAMbs Consult has a high success rate, no guarantee of recovery can be made.

7. Governing Law. This agreement is governed by applicable international financial law and the laws of the jurisdiction in which the Client resides.
