BAMbs Consult Service Agreement

This agreement is entered into between BAMbs Consult (BAc), a globally operating fund recovery consultancy, and the client named above (hereinafter "the Client").

1. Scope of Services. BAMbs Consult agrees to conduct a thorough investigation into the Client's reported financial loss, including fund tracing, regulatory liaison, legal coordination, and negotiation with financial institutions on behalf of the Client.

2. No Upfront Fees. BAMbs Consult does not charge any upfront, advance, or administrative fees. All fees are strictly contingency-based and are only applicable upon successful recovery of client funds.

3. Success Fee. Upon successful recovery, a success fee of 15% of the total recovered amount shall be payable to BAMbs Consult. This fee covers all investigative, legal, and administrative costs incurred during the recovery process.

4. Client Obligations. The Client agrees to provide accurate and complete information, cooperate fully with the investigation, and promptly supply any additional documentation requested by the case team.

5. Confidentiality. All information shared between the Client and BAMbs Consult shall remain strictly confidential and shall not be disclosed to any third party except where required by law or necessary for the execution of the recovery process.

6. No Guarantee. While BAMbs Consult has a high success rate, no guarantee of recovery can be made. The success of recovery depends on the specific circumstances of each case and the cooperation of third-party institutions.

7. Jurisdiction. This agreement is governed by applicable international financial law and the laws of the jurisdiction in which the Client resides.
