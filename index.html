Protect Yourself

Security & Awareness

Understanding how financial fraud works is your first line of defence. We educate victims and potential victims across all continents.

Recognise Fraud Patterns

Unrealistic returns, urgency pressure, unregistered brokers, and requests for personal banking credentials are all serious red flags.

Secure Your Data

Never share login details, PIN numbers, or OTP codes with anyone. Legitimate financial institutions will never ask for this information.

Document Everything

Keep records of all transactions, communications, and account statements. Evidence quality directly affects recovery success rates.

Report Immediately

The faster a case is reported, the higher the recovery rate. Contact BAc as soon as you suspect you have been defrauded.

Common Fraud Scenarios

  • Investment Platform Fraud — Fake trading platforms that display false profits, then refuse withdrawals. Often targeting forex, binary options, and crypto markets.
  • Romance Scam Finance — Online relationships that evolve into requests for money transfers to overseas accounts.
  • Phishing & Account Takeover — Fake emails or SMS messages that steal banking credentials to drain accounts.
  • Advance Fee Fraud — Promises of large returns or inheritances requiring upfront fees or taxes before release.
  • Crypto Wallet Scams — Fake wallet apps, seed phrase theft, and fake NFT platforms that drain crypto holdings.
  • Recovery Scams — Fraudulent agencies claiming to help recover funds while stealing more. Only use verified consultancies like BAc.
