Protect Yourself

Security & Awareness

Understanding how financial fraud works is your first line of defence. We educate victims and potential victims across all continents.

Recognise Fraud Patterns

Unrealistic returns, urgency pressure, unregistered brokers and requests for personal banking credentials are all major red flags.

Secure Your Data

Never share login details, PINs, or OTP codes with anyone. Legitimate institutions will never ask for this information.

Document Everything

Keep records of all transactions, communications and statements. Evidence quality directly affects recovery success rates.

Report Immediately

The faster a case is reported, the higher the recovery rate. Contact BAc as soon as you suspect fraud.

Common Fraud Scenarios Worldwide

  • Investment Platform Fraud — Fake trading platforms displaying false profits then refusing withdrawals. Targets forex, binary options and crypto.
  • Romance Scam Finance — Online relationships evolving into requests for money transfers to overseas accounts.
  • Phishing & Account Takeover — Fake emails or SMS messages stealing banking credentials to drain accounts.
  • Advance Fee Fraud — Promises of large returns requiring upfront fees before release of funds.
  • Crypto Wallet Scams — Fake wallet apps, seed phrase theft and fake NFT platforms draining crypto holdings.
  • Recovery Scams — Fraudulent agencies claiming to help while stealing more. Only use verified consultancies like BAc.
